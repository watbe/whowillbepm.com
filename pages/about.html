About This Site

This website was made out of the need for a quick and dirty way to look at all the polling data without bias while getting an accurate overview.

With the amount of variation in national polls, it can somtimes be difficult to get a brief, but accurate, view of the current political status, and most importantly, who will be leading Australia into 2014.

This website is a project by a student interested in politics. He can be contacted through email via wwbpm@lab273.com. Suggestions, feedback and other correspondence is welcome.

Methodology

The latest data from each company are aggregated as soon as they are updated. The Crikey BludgerTrack is used to smooth the data as their aggregation is analysed by statisticans, whereas this site does not attempt to. However, BludgerTrack does not update as soon as data comes out, so while their figures are used in the calculation, this site aims to improve this by delivering data as soon as they are available.

Sources can be found on this Google Spreadsheet. The numbers are rounded to 1 decimal place.

Disclaimer and Privacy

This site merely aggregates polls of leading research companies. It does not conduct research into voting intentions or behaviour of the public, and the accuracy of the predictions rely on the accuracy of the sources.

WhoWillBePM.com does not store personally identifiable information on visits or accesses to this website. Anonymised data is collected to provide statistics into the number of visits and nature of visits, such as where the visits are coming from. By visiting this website, you agree to the collection of anonymised data.
